This file will contain information about George Brown
college. George Brown College is a post-secondary school that offers various 
programs to students willing to continue their studies.
George Brown is known for Applied Arts and Technology, 
with three different campuses within Toronto.
There is the St.James campus,
the Casa Loma campus, and the Waterfront campus. George Brown College was 
chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year.
